Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how IPO Alert ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

a. Information Automatically Collected

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device Information: Hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: Details about how you interact with the App (e.g., IPOs viewed, notification settings).

b. Information Collected by Third Parties

The App includes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may use advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads.
  • Google Play Services: Used for core functionality and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality.
  • Send you notifications about IPO dates (if enabled).
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns.

3. Storage and Data Security

The App stores IPO data and your preferences locally on your device using an internal database. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR or CCPA to access or delete your data. As we do not store personal data on our servers, you can exercise these rights by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
